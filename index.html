Apply for Scholarship Program

Based on the Roads and Transport Authority’s keenness to attract and sponsor UAE National high school graduates to obtain academic qualifications that are in line with the requirements and needs of the Authority’s work in the labor market, scholarships have been provided to UAE national students to enroll in a few leading and accredited educational institutions within the country.

Terms and conditions to enroll in the program:

  • The applicant must be a national of the country.
  • To have obtained cumulative average of 3.5 for students continuing in third and fourth years in university studies.
  • Have an IELTS certificate or its equivalent as per the requirements of the university/educational institution.
  • To pass the personal interview successfully.
  • To be fit to work.
  • To be of good conduct and behavior according to a certificate from the competent authorities.
  • He must have completed national service or have an exemption (for males).
  • The candidate must be at least 18 years old to register in the Nafees program
  • The scholarship student and his guardian sign the scholarship contract and adhere to all the provisions mentioned therein.
  • Students of determination can also participate in the scholarship program
